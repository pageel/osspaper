# Contributing to OSSPaper

We welcome your research papers, technical analyses, and ebooks!

## How to Publish

1. **Fork this Repository**
2. **Create Your Author Directory**: `content/authors/your-github-username/`
3. **Copy Templates**: Use the templates found in `content/_templates/` for your paper or ebook.
4. **Write Content**:
   - Write in Markdown.
   - Images must be placed in `content/authors/your-github-username/assets/`.
   - **Image limit**: Must be under 500KB.
   - Allowed formats: `.md`, `.webp`, `.jpg`, `.png`. (No `.svg` or `.gif`).
5. **Open a Pull Request**: Submit your PR. Our Automated Safety Gate and AI Reviewer will verify your submission.
